About

Here are some details about hotels.

  • Hotel Bjelašnica

    Hotel Bjelašnica is set in 900 m from the Bjelašnica ski lift in Babin Do, 20 km from Sarajevo and 23 km from Jahorina. The hotel has a barbecue, terrace and sun terrace, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant. Ski equipment hire and bike hire are available at this hotel and the area is popular for skiing.

  • Hotel Han

    Located right next to the ski area of Bjelašnica, Hotel Han has a bus stop in front with direct links to Sarajevo centre. It offers rooms with LCD satellite TV and free Wi-Fi, free video-surveilled parking, and a restaurant with a large terrace.

  • Termag Hotel

    Situated at 1,550 m above sea level along the skiing tracks of Mount Jahorina, Termag Hotel Jahorina offers 2 restaurants and a wellness centre with an indoor pool, sauna and Turkish bath. The air-conditioned hotel provides free Wi-Fi, free private underground parking and there are ski instructors at your disposal.

  • Hotel Lavina

    Located just 30 m from the ski lift on Jahorina Mountain, Hotel Lavina has an indoor pool and spa facilities, as well as elegant accommodation units with free Wi-Fi. The hotel's stylish restaurant serves local specialities. LCD cable TV, telephone, safe and minibar are featured in all rooms and suites of the Lavina Hotel. Free parking is provided.

  • Hotel Blanca Resort & Spa

    Situated 26 km from Travnik at the foot of Mount Vlasic, Hotel Blanca Resort & Spa offers a spa and fitness area. The rooms feature free Wi-Fi and elegant modern furniture. The property is in the Babanovac ski centre at the foot of the former Olympic ski jumping venue and the skiing tracks.

  • Hotel Pahuljica

    This mountain hotel features a wellness centre, an indoor pool and an on-site ski school. It is 1,235 m above sea level, in prime position for both summer and winter activities. All guest rooms at Hotel Pahuljica are equipped with a spacious private bathroom with a shower. In addition, every room has satellite TV and a minibar.
